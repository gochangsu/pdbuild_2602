Usage Guide

This tool generates random numbers only. Each click produces a new 6-number set plus a bonus number.

  • Numbers are purely random and independent of past draws.
  • Results are for entertainment only and do not guarantee winnings.
  • Click again to generate another set.
FAQ

Do you use past draw data?

No. The generator is independent of any historical data.

Can I improve my odds?

All combinations have the same probability. Number patterns do not affect outcomes.

How is the bonus number chosen?

It is randomly selected from 1 to 45, excluding the main six.

Probability Notes

Each 6/45 combination is equally likely. Random selection helps avoid selection bias.

  • Consecutive or clustered numbers are not less likely.
  • Choosing “hot” numbers does not improve odds.
  • Non-duplicated picks are the core rule.
